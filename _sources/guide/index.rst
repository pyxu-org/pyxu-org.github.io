User Guide
##########

underlie Pyxu.  Whether you're curious about the assumptions that govern Pyxu objects, or interested in the inner
workings that make Pyxu an invaluable tool for computational imaging, this guide has it all. We'll walk you through the
essential aspects of the library, helping you harness its full power for your research or project.
